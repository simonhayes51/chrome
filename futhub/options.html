How to Use

  1. Connect: Click "Connect with Dashboard" above
  2. Premium: Ensure you have an active Premium subscription
  3. Visit FUT Web App: Go to the EA Sports FC Web App
  4. Sell Items: Sell players as normal
  5. Click "Record & Clear": Use the lime button that appears next to "Clear Sold"
  6. Check Dashboard: Trades automatically appear in your dashboard
